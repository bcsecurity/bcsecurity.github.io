Mission & Vision



Our mission is to secure every device by deploying various software and hardware to the end user, designed to be user friendly while protecting the user and their needs. We envision a future where every device is completely secure, creating a safer environment for everyone.

Who we are



Founded in 2014, Black Cat Security are an international group focused on educating the public about security and releasing tools to assist. We believe the future of society, need to be empowered and know that their actions can promote change. Education is the key to security online, and locally.
